Heuristics applied — register IDs

  • DIST-01 — NPA/auction source-labelled (ทรัพย์ KTB/BAM/GHB pill); never merged into generic "ขาย" stream
  • DIST-02 — LED/bank auction card shows 3 mandatory caveats inline (ขายตามสภาพ / อาจมีผู้อยู่อาศัย / ตรวจสอบโฉนด) — not collapsed (P4)
  • DIST-03 — urgency_indicator badge ("ขายด่วน") extracted and shown separately from NPA
  • DIST-04 — discount-to-market estimate shown on NPA cards ("↓ ~18% ต่ำกว่าตลาด")
  • DIST-09 — "ทรัพย์ NPA / ขายทอดตลาด" filter chip with active state; active-filter pill shows current filter
  • MKT-11 — listing_type includes foreclosure, new-build, resale as first-class values
  • COMP-05 — NPA filter chip exists in the filter rail (demanded by 51% of survey respondents, a5 F4)
  • COMP-06 — New-vs-resale filter chip ("ใหม่ / มือสอง") and card badges (demanded by 80% of survey respondents)
  • COPY-04 — deed-type/source badge visible on card without click-through
  • COPY-10 — "เจ้าของขายเอง" owner-direct badge (provenance, P2)
  • P4 — Honesty-by-default: price labelled "ราคาเปิดประมูล (ราคาประเมิน)" not market value; disclaimers not suppressed
  • P8 — Distressed supply tailwind handled with care: NPA is clearly segmented, not buried
  • TH-06/TH-07 — Sarabun body (looped Thai), line-height ≥1.65
  • TH-09/COMP-14 — 390px mobile-first frame; all cards legible at phone width
  • TECH-06 — Hex-first cascade + @supports OKLCH; badge tokens reference theme.css names verbatim
